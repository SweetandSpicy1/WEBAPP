Our Services:

  • Water Source Identification: Mapping natural water sources for better resource management.
  • Water Quality Monitoring: Real-time visualization of water quality data like pH, turbidity, and contamination levels.
  • Water Infrastructure Mapping: Visualizing pipelines, treatment plants, and tracking maintenance needs.
  • Water Accessibility Analysis: Identifying underserved regions and supporting equitable water distribution.
  • Community Reporting & Engagement: Platforms for user-reported water issues and transparency in water data.

Why Do We Build This Application

Waterwise aims to make water information accessible to everyone in Bukidnon, help protect our precious water resources by mapping and monitoring them, and support sustainable development in the province by providing a platform for informed decision-making about water use and conservation. .


By sharing this information, We hope to empower communities to take ownership of their water resources and work together to protect them for future generations. We also hope that this website will be a valuable tool for researchers and government agencies working to address water challenges in Bukidnon.
